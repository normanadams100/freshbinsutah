Do I need to be home?
No. Just leave the bins at the curb on your trash day like you already do. We handle the rest and text you when we're finished.
What about the wastewater?
Our rig captures 100% of the wastewater. Nothing runs into your gutter, lawn, or storm drain. We dispose of it properly — it's the whole reason a pro service exists instead of "just use a hose."
Is the cleaner safe for my dog and kids?
Yes. We use a biodegradable, plant-based deodorizer — no bleach, no harsh solvents. The bins are safe to touch the moment we leave.
How hot is the water, really?
Up to 200°F at 3,000 PSI. That's what actually kills bacteria and breaks down baked-on residue. A garden hose maxes at 80°F and 40 PSI. It isn't the same thing.
Can I cancel the subscription?
Anytime. No fees, no scripts, no guilt trip. Text us and it's done.
Do you clean recycling and compost bins too?
Yes. Same price, same process. Recycling bins are often worse than trash because of the sugary residue that nobody rinses out first.
