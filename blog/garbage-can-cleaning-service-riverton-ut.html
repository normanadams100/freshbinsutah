What Is a Garbage Can Cleaning Service?

A garbage can cleaning service (also called a trash bin cleaning service, garbage can washing service, or bin wash service) is a mobile operation that comes to your home after trash pickup and cleans your empty cans on the spot. The cleaning truck carries its own water, heats it to roughly 200°F, and uses high-pressure jets to blast out residue, bacteria, maggots, and odor-causing grime from every inside surface of the bin.

The wastewater is captured inside the truck. None of it runs into your driveway or storm drain. After the pressure wash, the bin is deodorized and sanitized with an eco-friendly solution that is safe around kids and pets.

The whole process takes about 3 to 5 minutes per cart.

Why Hire a Professional Garbage Bin Cleaning Service in Riverton?

You can clean a trash can yourself with a garden hose, a scrub brush, and some bleach. Plenty of Riverton homeowners do exactly that once or twice a year. Here's why hiring it out makes more sense:

1. Water Temperature

A garden hose tops out around 60°F. Commercial bin cleaning equipment runs at 180 to 200°F, which is the temperature range that actually kills E. coli, Salmonella, Listeria, and the fly larvae that breed in warm-weather bins.

2. Wastewater

When you clean a can in your driveway, the runoff, full of rotten food, bacteria, and whatever cleaning chemicals you used, flows into the street and eventually into local waterways. Riverton storm drains are not filtered. A professional garbage can cleaning company captures 100% of the wastewater for proper disposal.

3. The Smell

Utah summers are the real test. A can that gets cleaned monthly does not develop the baked-on film that causes the smell in the first place.

4. Your Back

The average residential cart weighs 30+ pounds empty and is awkward to tip, scrub, and rinse. For older homeowners or anyone with a bad back, this alone is worth the price.
